# Code of Conduct

AISOP practice spaces should support serious technical disagreement without personal attacks.

Participants are expected to:

- focus criticism on artifacts, claims, tests, and evidence;
- preserve attribution and revision history;
- avoid publishing private information, secrets, or unsafe instructions without context;
- respect maintainers' safety, security, and licensing gates.

Maintainers may remove content that is abusive, unsafe, spammy, deceptive, or unrelated to the project purpose.
